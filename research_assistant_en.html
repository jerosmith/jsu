Research Assistance

I help you focus on your core research topics while I do the time-consuming tasks for you:
    1. Extract data from various sources such as the World Bank, OECD and Eurostat, and assemble them into a tidy format ready for analysis.
    2. Generate a variety of appealing bespoke graphs and plots for an exploratory data analysis.
    3. Perform linear regression for many models.
    4. Proofread your papers, ensuring they are written in good academic English. English is my native language and I have published several scientific papers in peer-reviewed journals.
    5. Perform administrative duties and coordinate relationships with other organisations and suppliers.
Because I do the data analysis programmatically using the R programming language, and not on a one-by-one basis, I can produce hundreds or thousands of graphs or model estimates, iterating over different values of control parameters. Some examples of the power of this approach are the seminal paper of Granger and Newbold in 1974: "Spurious Regression in Econometrics" and "I Just Ran Two Million Regressions" by Sala-I-Martin (1997).
